Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for housing?

Simply log in with your student credentials and follow the step-by-step application process. You can apply for up to 2 on-campus and unlimited off-campus residences.

When will I know if I got housing?

You'll receive real-time updates on your application status. Most decisions are made within 24-48 hours of application.

What if I don't get my first choice?

Our system automatically considers your preferences and places you on waitlists. You'll be notified immediately when your preferred option becomes available.
